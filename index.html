Velvet Music

Skills used
  • UX
  • UI
  • Documentation
  • Illustrator
  • Photoshop
  • Microsoft Word

In this project we worked as a team (5) and did a research based on a given casus. Thanks to the theoretical information we got from the book Seductive Interaction Design written by Stephen P. Anderson we were able to make a redesign for this given casus. Every process through this project has been documentated.

The casus for this project is about an online musicstore named Velvet Music. Velvet has a wide product range and thus sells products of different music genres. In order to get an optimalized product, we had to focus on a specific music genre. The delivered product contains a redesign of the homepage and detailpage based on the specific chosen music genre.

Online webshop of Velvet Music does not require the expectations and has to be redesigned. Our goal was to optimalise the store experience of customers. In order to achieve our goal we had to chose a certain target audience.

One of my main roles in our projectteam was to make sure the detailpage meets the requirements we set up as a team. The requirements are based on the 52 principle Mental Notes.

Looye Kwekers

Skills used
  • UX
  • UI
  • Documentation
  • Illustrator
  • Photoshop
  • Microsoft Word

We have been asked as a projectgroup (5) to develop an online campaign for the dutch tomato grower factory Looye Kwekers.

The online campaign needs to connect Looye Kwekers with her target group. Looye has also a great goal to achieve. In 2025, 33 out of 1000 people inside the target group must buy at least 1 product of Looye every month. Setting this goal for herself, Looye wants to become the best selled and loved tomato grower in The Netherlands, Germany and Belgium.

Our solution was to create a way that not only Looye sells her products, achieves her goal and has a strong bonding with her target group. We also tried to find a way in order to fulfill the wishes and needs of the target group.

After some research we came to the conclusion that we had to design a way in order to help the target group to eat healthier and in a way that will fill up to their own lifestyle.

My biggest role in this project group was to do deskreseach and fieldresearch about the target group. Nevertheless to design some of the final products.
